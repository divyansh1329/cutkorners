Why CutKorners

Don't buy | Simply rent

In this fast changing world, “access is the new ownership”. CutKorners is India’s first consumer appliances renting site. Our concept is based on that of shared economy, which makes furnishing your home easy, quick and affordable. As part of our service we rent out household consumer durables including TV, Refrigerator, Washing Machine and Microwave on a monthly subscription fee. More categories to be launched soon. You can choose from one of our packages also(i.e. Bronze, Silver, Gold or Platinum) . The service also includes doorstep delivery and installation making it extremely convenient and hassle-free to furnish your home. Our service promises deliveries within 3 days of the order being placed with the flexibility to swap/return based on requirement. Should a fault arise, we guarantee servicing/exchange of your product within 48 hours, or the monthly rent is on us.
